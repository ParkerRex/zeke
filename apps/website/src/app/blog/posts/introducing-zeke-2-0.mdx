We're excited to announce ZEKE 2.0 - a complete reimagining of research intelligence. With AI-powered insights, real-time collaboration, and lightning-fast performance, managing your research has never been easier.

<br />

## AI-Powered Research Assistant
![AI Assistant](/images/blog/ai-assistant.jpg)
<br />
Our new AI assistant can help you analyze research papers, extract key insights, and generate summaries in seconds. Simply upload your documents and let ZEKE do the heavy lifting.

#### Smart Summarization
Get instant summaries of research papers with key findings, methodology, and conclusions highlighted automatically.

#### Citation Management
Never lose track of your sources again. Our AI automatically extracts and formats citations in any style you need.

<br />
<br />

## Real-Time Collaboration
![Collaboration](/images/blog/collaboration.jpg)
<br />
Work seamlessly with your team on research projects. Share insights, annotate documents together, and stay in sync with real-time updates.

#### Live Editing
Multiple team members can work on the same document simultaneously with zero conflicts.

#### Smart Notifications
Get notified when team members add insights, comments, or make important discoveries.

#### Version History
Track every change with our comprehensive version history. Roll back to any previous version with a single click.

<br />
<br />

## Lightning-Fast Search
![Search](/images/blog/search.jpg)
<br />
Find exactly what you're looking for across all your research materials. Our new search engine is 10x faster and understands natural language queries.

#### Semantic Search
Search by concept, not just keywords. Ask "studies about climate change impact on agriculture" and get relevant results instantly.

#### Filter by Anything
Date ranges, authors, document types, tags - filter your research library however you need.
